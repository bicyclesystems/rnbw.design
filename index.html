rnbw can hook artificial intelligence to understand your project and provides you with a range of tools to manage it. ask  things and let ai do things for you.

rnbw is a design tool that “get you” - let digital mind do the work and unleash your creativity! ai-powered commands allows you to focus on creating.

we’re not in canvas anymore

html, css and js are not just code, or what we see in the browser. they're design tools. when designing with them, we're not on a flat canvas, we're crafting within the web's natutal structure. and it's a whole different thing!

freely move and compose content. with rnbw, you're designing inside the web's framework. working within its limits, you're embracing the essence of automatic layouts where it performs best - the native web.

simple yet dangerous

simple enough for anyone, flexible enough for anything, and open enough to use anyhow. rnbw is focusing on atomic concepts. when combined, they multiply their meaning. rnbw is designed to fracture.

rnbw allows users to build their solutions in the best way possible that works for them, not others. we believe this design principle is the key to more powerful software, and software for creating software.

your files, who knew?

that's right, rnbw is local-first software as it lets you open and edit any file type like html, css, markdown, javascript, directly within the platform.

experience seamless, user-friendly editing as you visually manipulate your files and bring your projects to life.

design at your command

rnbw is prompt-driven and optimized for you and your keyboard. the command menus are designed to bring you that feeling of lightness and flow.

keyboard shorcuts are the de-facto way of doing things in rnbw. hit J for the jumpstart menu. hit A to add something. hit W to do something. you've just learned it!

design together, the dev way

finally, embrace git and async collaboration. but there's more. a lot more. continuous integration tests design elements in their natural environment, issue tracking keeps everyone updated, and code reviews ensure quality.

we believe in fully embracing dev workflows to integrate design and development. it's individual effort for collective gain! adopting these methods elevates both dev and design, making the team more cohesive and efficient.
