GDPR Compliance Statement

Effective Date April 30, 2023

rnbw is committed to protecting the privacy of its users and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) guidelines. The GDPR is a European Union regulation that establishes a framework for protecting the personal data of individuals within the EU.

Based on the information provided about rnbw, the platform does not collect, process, or store any personal data of its users. Therefore, the GDPR may not apply to rnbw directly. However, as a responsible software provider, we still wish to outline our commitment to privacy and GDPR principles.

Data Collection and Processing

rnbw does not collect or process any personal information about its users. The platform is designed to work with local files and data, and no information is uploaded to rnbw's servers.

Data Security

As a user of rnbw, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your local files and data. rnbw does not store your project files or data on its servers.

Local Browser Storage

rnbw may store some information in your local browser storage to remember recently opened projects. This information is limited in scope and does not include any personal data.

User Consent

rnbw requires user permission to read data from the local computer inside the browser. By granting this permission, you consent to rnbw accessing the specified files and data for the purpose of providing its services.

Third-Party Services

rnbw may include links to third-party services, such as extensions or plugins. It is your responsibility to ensure that these third-party services comply with GDPR requirements if you choose to use them in conjunction with rnbw.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our GDPR compliance or privacy practices, please feel free to contact us.
